Here at Ruffian Supplies, we care for our dogs.

Why do we only have one canine friend? Two reasons:

Ethically sourced dog food, to keep our furry friends healthy!
And to help them catch all the squirrels in the park.
Our products are paw-chosen by our in-house canine friend, Scarlet.
Since they are picked by a real dog, you know they are the best and healthiest treats!

Scarlet was adopted from the animal shelter at 1 year, 8 months old.


She was given up by her old owners, but not much else is known about Scarlet's past.
She was taken to her new home while she had pneumonia, so she needed a lot of antibiotics.
After about a weeks time though, she beat it, and was ready to be a dog again!
Now she enjoys her time going on plenty of walks, and having lots of treats!
She also likes the beach and the park a lot. But no squirrels or cats are allowed past her.
